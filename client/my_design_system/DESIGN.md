# Design System Overview

This document outlines the core visual principles and tokenized values that define our digital products.

## Theme Configuration

**Color Mode:** dark

**Color Palette:**
Our brand utilizes a distinct color palette to establish visual hierarchy and brand identity.
- **Primary Color:** `#e39212` - A bold, vibrant orange, serving as the most distinctive chromatic color for key interactive elements like buttons and calls to action.
- **Secondary Color:** `#976f3f` - A warm, earthy brown, supporting less prominent UI elements, chips, and secondary actions.
- **Tertiary Color:** `#1faef0` - A bright, clear blue, acting as an additional accent for highlights, badges, or decorative elements.
- **Neutral Color:** `#81756a` - A soft, muted greyish-brown, forming the base for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements.

## Typography

**Headline Font:** Inter - Used for all major headings and titles to ensure strong visual impact and readability.
**Body Font:** Inter - Applied to all paragraph text and general content, prioritizing legibility and a consistent reading experience.
**Label Font:** Inter - Utilized for form labels, small annotations, and interactive element labels, maintaining clarity and consistency with other text elements.

## Shape and Form

**Roundedness:** 2 (Moderate) - UI shapes feature a moderate degree of corner rounding, providing a balanced and approachable aesthetic that is neither too sharp nor overly soft.

## Spacing

**Spacing:** 2 (Normal) - A normal level of whitespace is applied within and between UI elements, ensuring a comfortable visual balance and good readability without feeling too dense or overly spacious.
